KOORA BINATNA

Sunday April 11, 2021 our student club "Mimouna" of Al Akhawayn University of Ifrane organized a cultural and sports day under the theme: "KOORA BINATNA" and this, with the participation of students of the Maimonide school of Casablanca and members of the Moroccan Association of former box starts.
On the program of this day dedicated to tolerance and interreligious dialogue, especially between Moroccan Jews and Muslims, a direct interview with members of the Moroccan Association of Former Stars of Moroccan Boxing who highlighted the very important role played by the Moroccan Boxing. sport of Boxing in the rapprochement between peoples without racial or religious distinction as well as the role played by Moroccan Boxers of Jewish faith with at their head the former champion of the Arab world Max Cohen and the former champion Schlomo Attias who have were able to hoist the national colors very high in the sky of the international sports scene alongside their Moroccan compatriots of Muslim faith like the former world champion of military boxing, the boxer Ait Abbou and the medalist of 'gold in 1957 in Lebanon, boxers Mohamed Benchikh (Chipo).

Also, the day was marked by the organization of an exhibition Football match which opposed the students of Al Akhawayn University of Ifrane to the students of the Maimonide School of Casablanca, ping-pong matches. and parts of chess games.
The closing ceremony of the day was marked by the presentation of the trophy to the winning team and the presentation of packages to the students of the Maimonide school.
